Converting Knowledge Bases to GeneXus 16, 17 or higher

Depending on which is the initial GeneXus version and to what target GeneXus version you are about to move, GeneXus does some conversion steps on your KB and you need to follow some particular steps too.

From GeneXus 16 to GeneXus 17

There is no KB conversion from 16 to 17.

Anyway, note that there may not be a way back. A KB that uses GeneXus Access Manager (GAM), once it is opened or built with GeneXus 17 cannot be built by GeneXus 16.
Furthermore, the information saved by some objects in GeneXus 17 (eg.: Query object) cannot be read accurately by GeneXus 16.

More information: GeneXus 17 Compatibility Section.

From GeneXus 15 to GeneXus 16

There is no KB conversion from 15 to 16.

More Information: GeneXus 16 Compatibility Section, GeneXus 16 is easy to adopt

From GeneXus X Evolution 3 or previous versions to GeneXus 16, 17, or higher

From GeneXus 9 on, you can open any KB with GeneXus 16, and GeneXus will do an automatic conversion process on it.

Note that you don't need to open it with each intermediate version, but anyway check the following documents for particular points to take into account for your case:

  • GeneXus 17 Compatibility Section
  • GeneXus 16 Compatibility Section
  • GeneXus 15 Compatibility Section
  • GeneXus X Evolution 3 Compatibility Section
  • GeneXus X Evolution 2 Compatibility Section
  • Converting a KB From GeneXus 9.0 (or prior)

For any conversion process, it is advisable to perform an objects navigation comparison.
